Pokémon and Video Games

My name is Jose Caridad, I've been playing video games since I was 4 years old, and since then I have not stopped. It's really fun to play everyday and I feel like I go to somewhere else everytime I'm playing, but not everything is as good as it seems. Playing video games sometimes separates you from your loved ones and can become an addiction, a really bad one. It took me years to realize that, it was affecting my grades and my interpersonal relationships. After a while, I learned to control the time I spent playing and became a better person, realized how valuable my friends are, and also started to work for everything I want to become in the future. I still play a lot of video games, and I love it, but I know how to balance my life better, for me and for the ones that surround me.
